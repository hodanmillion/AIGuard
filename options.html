PII_MVP setup

Just fill in a few basics and we’ll quietly watch for PII in AI chats.

1. Alert destination

Paste your Google Apps Script webhook URL that sends email to your security/shared inbox.

Dummy example: https://script.google.com/macros/s/YOUR-ID/exec

2. Who is this browser?

Use an email, username, or device label like FrontDesk-PC-01.

3. Strictness

Start in “Warn only” if you want people to get used to it, then move to “Block & alert”.

4. Company label (optional)

This label just shows up in alerts, handy if you manage multiple environments.

Tip: roll out in “Warn only” for a week before switching to “Block & alert”.
